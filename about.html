Atithi Canteen

A smart canteen ordering system built for students, by students.

About

Atithi Canteen is a digital ordering platform designed to make canteen experience faster and hassle-free. Students can browse the menu, place orders, select payment method, and give feedback — all from their phone or browser. No more waiting in long queues!
